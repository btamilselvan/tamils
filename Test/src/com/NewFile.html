Single Sign On Help

This is my first time to JENIE (Single Sign On).

At this point you need to self activate to establish your JENIE account. To do this, please follow this Self Activation link. When you are done with this step, you will be directed back to the log in page.

This is my first time signing into JENIE (Single Sign On).

At this point you have self activated and/or changed your system generated password to establish your JENIE account. If you have not self activated, please follow this Self Activation link.

I have forgotten my username.

Your username is typically your FirstName LastName (Example: John Smith). User names may also contain initials, a middle name or an appended number (Examples: John W Smith, John W. Smith, J William Smith, J Smith or John Smith1).

If you cannot log in because you have lost or forgotten your username please use the automated process by clicking the Forgot Username link.

I need to reset my password?

If you do not remember your password, or your password is not working, you can reset your password to something you know. To do this, on the log-in screen enter your Username, leave the password field blank, and click the Forgot Password link.The challenge questions you selected during self activation will be displayed. Once you have answered the questions, you will be prompted to change your password.

I need to contact the JENIE Help Desk.

If you have any questions, please contact the JENIE Help Desk at: 210-301-6322 or via email at jenie@support.aotx.uscourts.gov.

Access to this system is limited to use by only those authorized by the United States Courts. If evidence of unauthorized or criminal activity is detected, systems personnel may provide that evidence to law enforcement officials.
